Privacy Policy — Xmas Countdown

Last updated: August 27, 2025

Thank you for using Xmas Countdown (the “App”). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

No personal data is collected. The App does not ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.

The App may store minimal, non-personal preferences locally on your device (for example: theme selection, notification settings, or whether you purchased “Remove Ads”). These preferences never leave your device unless stated below.

2. Permissions Used

  • No special device permissions are required for the basic countdown functionality.
  • If optional features are added later (e.g., notifications), the App will request only the minimum permission necessary and will clearly explain the purpose in-app.

3. Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertisements. Ads can be provided by third-party partners (e.g., Google AdMob). These partners may use device identifiers and cookies to deliver, measure, and improve ads in accordance with their own privacy policies.

  • You can choose “Remove Ads” as a one-time in-app purchase; when active, ads are disabled.
  • Learn more about how Google uses data: Google — How Google uses information
  • Manage your Google ad settings: adssettings.google.com

4. In-App Purchases

The App may offer a one-time in-app purchase (e.g., Remove Ads). Purchases are processed by Google Play. The App does not receive or store your payment card details. Google may process limited data necessary to complete the transaction in accordance with Google’s policies.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is designed for a general audience and does not target children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data to third parties. If ads are enabled, limited data may be processed by advertising partners solely for ad delivery and measurement as described above.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect any settings stored by the App. Remember that no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we add or change features (for example, introducing notifications or analytics). Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.
